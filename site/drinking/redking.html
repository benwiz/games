Red King

7, 8: Peeker - Peek at your own

9, 10: Sneaker - Peed at someone else's

J, Q: Blind swap - Swap any two cards on the table without peeking

K: Looking swap - Swap any two cards on the table but you can look at the cards you select

Drinking Rules

7, 8: Peeker - You drink (1)

9, 10: Sneaker - Target drinks (2)

J, Q: Blind swap - Targets drink, may include yourself (2)

K: Looking swap - Targets drink, may include yourself (2)

You win race with your own card: Give drinks (2)

You win race with your someone else's card: Give drinks (2), target takes drinks (2)

Blunder (perform illegal action): You drink (finish)

Late to the race: You drink (2)

Wrong to the race: You drink (half)

Late and wrong to the race: You drink (finish)

Pay to knock: You drink (2)

Lose after knocking: You drink (finish)

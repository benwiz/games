Red King

7, 8: Peeker - Peek at your own

9, 10: Sneaker - Peek at someone else's

J, Q: Blind swap - Swap any two cards on the table without peeking

K: Looking swap - Swap any two cards on the table but you can look at the cards you select

Drinking Rules

7, 8: Peeker - You drink

9, 10: Sneaker - Target drinks

J, Q: Blind swap - Targets drink, may include yourself

K: Looking swap - Targets drink, may include yourself

You win race with your own card: Give drinks

You win race with your someone else's card: Give drinks, target takes drinks

Blunder (perform illegal action): You drink (finish)

Late to the race: You drink

Wrong to the race: You drink a lot

Late and wrong to the race: You drink a lot more

Pay to knock: You drink

Lose after knocking: You drink a lot
